Welcome to our FNMI Resource Guide!

We decided we would provide an interactive map as our resource guide that provided a brief look into the relationship that Indigenous peoples share with water, and more specifically their endurance of the constant water-boiling advisories and lack of access to clean water. By zooming in to each point, the reader can gain a better understanding into the locational context of each of the communities, including the natural and built environment surrounding these communities. Clicking the markers will bring up a breif description on each location, and every location has a "More Details" link at the bottom, which will bring up a new page with further information.
